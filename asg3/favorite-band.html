My Favorite Band: Block-B

Introduction

Block B is a South Korean boy band that debuted in 2011. It consists of seven members: Taeil, B-Bomb, Jaehyo, U-Kwon, Park Kyung, Zico, and PO. Block B has received numerous honors, including seven Golden Disc Awards and eleven Seoul Music Award nominations.
