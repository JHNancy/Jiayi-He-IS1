Wanda Maximoff

Wanda Maximoff(The Scarlet Witch)In Marvel Cinematic Universe

Introduction

She is a female hero in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) film franchise, primarily portrayed by Elizabeth Olsen. Wanda is a Sokovian refugee who, along with her twin brother Pietro Maximoff, volunteered for Hydra experiments. After coming into contact with the Mind Stone and harnessing chaos magic, she gained telekinesis and energy manipulation abilities. She later joined the Avengers, helping them defeat Ultron and becoming one of their most powerful members.

Powers She Have

  • Psionics:

    • Wanda's spiritual energy came from contact with Loki's scepter, which contained the Mind Stone. She can use telekinesis to lift heavy objects and fly, and can use psionic powers for mental manipulation, reading minds and communicating telepathically.

  • Magic:

    • Chaos magic grants the user the ability to warp time and reality, and only works when reality is unstable and chaotic. Chaos magic is considered the most powerful form of magic.
