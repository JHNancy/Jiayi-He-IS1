My Favorite Movies

Summary: The movies I've listed are all that I've watched at least three times. Although they aren't all the same genre, they all evoked strong emotions in me while watching them. They are all excellent films and have helped me become a more thoughtful person. (No specific ranking)

The Avengers

This film tells the story of how, after Thanos snapped his fingers and wiped out half of Earth's population, the surviving Avengers found a way to bring them back. They all returned to Earth to seek revenge against Thanos's army.

The Rise of the Planet of the Apes

This film series tells the story of a time after a sibling infection outbreak that killed most of humanity. The apes began to evolve, developing the ability to talk and establish their own habitats, leading to a series of stories and conflicts with the surviving humans.

Fast & Furious

This is a series of action and car chase movies that also explore the bonds between friends and family. The seventh installment is my favorite; it contains many classic scenes and the iconic song "See You Again."

Kingsman

The first two films in this series tell the story of a secret, independent intelligence agency recruiting and training the protagonist into a highly skilled agent, who then develops a series of strategies to fight against the villain's global conspiracy.

The Maze Runner

This film series tells the story of a group of experimental subjects who are placed into a world designed by a secret organization, with the only exit being a giant moving maze inhabited by giant spiders. They keep trying to escape, but the outside world has also been corrupted.

X-Men

This film series tells the story of a group of people with superpowers who have their own school and are known as mutants. The school's principal selects mutants from among them to form the X-Men to fight various villains.

Minions

This film series mainly tells the story of yellow organisms gradually evolving into Minions and their journey to find an evil master, eventually finding Gru to become their master and helping Gru defeat other evil characters.

Kung Fu Panda

This movie tells the story of Po the panda who is chosen by Master Oogway to be the new "Dragon Warrior". Through continuous practice and training, as well as his own understanding, he finally becomes the "Dragon Warrior" in everyone's hearts.

Monsters University

This movie tells the story of a university full of monsters with a scare major, and Mike and Sullivan who are two main characters and go from enemies to friends. Their common goal is to help their team succeed and become scaresters.

Ratatouille

This movie tells the story of a rat with an extraordinary sense of taste and smell who inadvertently helps a male protagonist who can't cook. Inside the protagonist's chef's hat, the rat manipulates him to create many delicious dishes. Finally, it successfully opens a restaurant as the head chef with other characters, because anyone can cook.

Coco

This film tells the story of a young boy who accidentally falls into the world of the dead, where people disappear completely if they are not remembered by the living. There, he encounters his deceased family members and embarks on a journey to find his real grandfather and the truth.

Up

This film tells the story of 78-year-old Carl who missing his wife and doesn't want the government to demolish their house. So he builds a flying house out of countless balloons and embarks on the adventure he and his wife promised each other. Along the way, he encounters a little boy and a dog, and together they successfully reach the place of his dreams.
